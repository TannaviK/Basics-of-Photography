Contrast

Contrast is an attribute by which photographers can define their subjects better.
There are two differing types - tonal and colour contrast.
Tonal contrast is the juxstaposition of the various tones (between white, grey, & black) in the image.
Colour contrast is the way certain colours in the image off-set others. Usually warmer values off-set cooler colours, but not always.
This requires a lot of pre-meditative decisions of the composition of the picture for the best portrayal.
